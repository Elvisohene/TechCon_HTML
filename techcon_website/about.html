HISTORY

TechCon is an annual technology conference that started in 2018. It started as a small conference but grew to reach a worldwide audience after just two years.

In 2018 the first conference was hosted by Unversity of Vegas, Las Vegas.

MISSION

TechCon conference was organised to ;

  • To showcase new Technology innovatoins.
  • To host workshops.
  • To update the world on what's going on in the tech world.
